History of the Company

Since 2021, AMCC has excelled in electrical and mechanical solutions.

Since 2021, AMCC Engineers & Services (Pvt.) Ltd has offered comprehensive services including installations, EPC projects, solar power plants, consulting, design, engineering, workshop manufacturing, and industrial products. Our commitment to excellence, personalized service, and stringent safety standards ensures superior outcomes for every project.

During this time, AMCC has become specialized in planned preventative maintenance as well as installations. Our success is based on continuous commitment to clients, constant service development, competitive pricing, experienced mechanical and electrical engineers, fast reliable 24-hour call-service, and preventative maintenance.
